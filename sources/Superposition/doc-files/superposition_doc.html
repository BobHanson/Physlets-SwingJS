Superposition Embedding

Superposition models the superposition of two traveling waves.

Superposition is embedded using the following html tag:

<applet
  code="superposition.Superposition.class"
  archive="Superposition4_.jar,STools4.jar"
  codebase="../classes"
  name="Superposition"
  width="400"
  height="300">

<param name="numGraphs" value="3" />
<param name="pixPerX" value="20" />
<param name="pixPerY" value="10" />
<param name="gridX" value="1" />
<param name="gridY" value="1" />
<param name="func1" value="2*sin(pi*x/2-pi*t)" />
<param name="func2" value="2*sin(pi*x/2+pi*t)" />
<param name="showControls" value="true" />
<param name="FPS" value="10" />
<param name="dt" value="0.1" />
</applet>
