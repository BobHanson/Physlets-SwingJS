Wavepacket Time Development

The QTime Physlet displays the probability density of the wavepacket along with the quantum phase. The thickness of the wavepacket at any value of x is proportional to the amplitude of the wavefunction.

Instructions

Click on the problem number to initialize the simulation at the top of the page. You may start and stop the simulations using the buttons on the bottom of the animation. Click and drag inside the animation to read the coordinates and probability.
